How to Play

  • Choose your avatar and save your profile before creating or joining a room.
  • In Classic mode, only the active player can flip cards on their turn.
  • In Time Attack mode, everyone can flip cards as fast as they can until the board is cleared.
  • The selected mode, room updates, and system messages are shown to everyone in the room.
